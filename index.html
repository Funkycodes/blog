The Journey to Mediocrity at Low level Programming

LOW LEVEL PROGRAMMING, CAREER

Stepping Up

So February this year I was admitted to a cohort based software engineering bootcamp that promised to teach every thing needed to get started in any part of the software engineering stacks.

I have to admit that the first month was hell and I was so nervous and scared that I wasn't going to make it through the month. It is also worthy of mention that during this period I was diagnosed with ADHD, this caused me to suffer bouts of extreme anxiety and hyperfocus, this combined with the fact that I wass already struggling with OCD(Obsessive-compulsive disorder) made things very hard indeed. Fast forward 3 months and I have only great things to say about the program. The learning is done in a project based manner. This leads to students being forced to learn fast or get left behind.
